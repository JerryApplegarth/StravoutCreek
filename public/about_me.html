Hi there! My name is Jerry Applegarth and welcome to my mining site.

The year was 2002 when this 79 acre claim became available to file on as a mining claim.

I had been doing recreational prospecting all my life. It's something that has been a great interest to me. I obtained the claim and have been doing recreational mining ever since that day.

Summer Mining

Because this is a family recreational event for us and the fact that BLM prefers that we mine only durning the summer months, our mining usually run from May through September. This is because during the fall and winter months fish need the waters to be very clean for reproduction.

We only go to the claim on weekends and long holidays during the summer months. This claim is a 'placer claim', meaning that we are working the gravel bars for gold that has been washed downstream in the creek.

Nuggets

Most gold that is found in any placer claim are smaller pieces often less than one gram. Sometimes you get lucky and find bigger pieces but not often.
